FCDO / British International Investment (BII, formerly CDC)

Investor Sentiment Index: South African Energy Sector

Completed · 2022

The Project

Context & Objectives

FCDO and BII (British International Investment) commissioned ITAD to evaluate their efforts to mobilise private investment in South Africa's energy sector. CASM Technology built the NLP analysis module — a sentence-level sentiment index tracking how investor sentiment in South Africa's energy market shifted over 25 years, and identifying the key drivers of those shifts.

My Role

Contributions

Technical Lead. I designed and built the full NLP pipeline from scratch — from investment relevance classification through to sentiment detection and driver identification. I also defined the sentiment framework, distinguishing genuine investor sentiment from stock market noise.

This was a technically novel approach: sentence-level rather than article-level analysis, which allowed finer-grained sentiment detection with fewer labelled instances.

Method

Approach & Pipeline

  1. Data collection — 80,622 articles from 17 online news sources (1996–2022)
  2. Keyword annotators — 7 keyword filters for South Africa relevance and investment relevance
  3. Investment relevance classifier — articles split into chunks, transformer classifier trained (F1: 71%)
  4. Sentiment relevance classifier — trained on investment chunks (F1: 68%)
  5. Energy sector filtering — energy keyword annotators applied to relevant chunks
  6. Sentence splitting — articles split to sentence level for fine-grained analysis
  7. Positive sentiment classifier — trained on 500 manually annotated sentences (F1: 89%)
  8. Negative sentiment classifier — trained on 500 manually annotated sentences (F1: 80%)
  9. Driver identification — 500 sentences manually analysed → 5 primary drivers: Economic, Political, Enabling Environment, Investor Priorities, COVID-19
  10. Driver classifiers — 5 binary classifiers trained (Enabling Environment: 81%, Economics: 82%, Politics: 88%, Investor Priorities: 78%, COVID-19: 93%)
  11. Validation — sentiment index validated as predictive of net investment flows in South Africa

Outcomes

Results & Impact

Sentiment index covering 25 years of South African energy investment discourse. 7 classifiers trained across relevance, sentiment and driver detection.

Key finding: the sentiment index is predictive of net investment flows — the strongest validation signal across all markets studied in the evaluation.

5 primary drivers and 14 sub-drivers of investment sentiment identified. Work referenced in a published ITAD blog post (March 2023).
